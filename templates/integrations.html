Integrations

Fasten integrates with any app, any time, anywhere. Below are some examples and steps on how to configure several services to use Fasten

Github Stripe Trello

Github

  1. Go to your repo settings by click on the settings tab on the right sidebar.

  2. Once in settings, click on "Service Hooks" on the left sidebar.

  3. Now click on "WebHook URLs" in the middle column.

  4. Input your Fasten webhook url into the input box and click "Update Settings"

  5. Now go put your Fasten embed code into your app and go brag about it!

Stripe

  1. Once logged into your Stripe account, go to your Webhook Settings

  2. Click the "Add URL" button on the bottom right of the popup.

  3. Input your Fasten webhook url into the input box and click "Create webhook URL"

  4. Now go put your Fasten embed code into your app and go brag about it!

Trello

  1. Since Trello has implemented its webhook system without a UI, you will need to see the Trello webhook docs in order to set up your Fasten hook in Trello.

  2. Once the webhook is set up in Trello, you put your Fasten embed code into your app and go brag about it!
