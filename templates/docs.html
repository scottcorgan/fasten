Creating a new hook

  1. Head on over the your hooks dashboard.

  2. Click on "Create hook"

  3. Fill in the following information for the hook:

    • Title - a human readable title that helps you find the hook within the dashboard. This is private to you and never made public.
    • Endpoint - the path of your webhook that uniquely identifies it. This becomes the end if the full webhook url.
    • Domains - comma delimmed white list of domains. These will be the domains that are allowed to access this webhook.

  4. Click "Create hook" and go brab about it!

Embedding the hook into your frontend/static app

  1. Include the Fasten script into your app anywhere you'd like:

    <script src="http://www.fasten.io/fasten.js"></script>

  2. Use the following code—anywhere after the previous script tag—to connect to that webhook over a web socket:

    var fasten = Fasten.connect('your/fasten/hook/path');
 
fasten.hook(function (data) {
  // Do amazing stuff here!
});

Changing your password

If, at any time, you need to change your password, you can go to settings and show that password who's boss!

Deleting your account

If by chance the world is ending and you need to quickly delete your account, you can easily go to settings and click "Delete my account". But, it won't be something to brag about.
